My Future Career

I aspire to become a Data Scientist working in the tech industry. Data Science involves extracting insights from complex and unstructured data to help organizations make strategic decisions. It offers a competitive salary, great growth opportunities, and the chance to work on cutting-edge technologies.

According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a Data Scientist in the United States is $120,000 per year. The field of Data Science is rapidly growing, with a high demand for skilled professionals across various industries.
